About us

Providing efficient, effective, and
coordinated humanitarian response
to Nigerians.

The National Social Investment Programs of Nigeria is a
social walfare initiative created by the Federal
government of Nigeria in 2015 under the direction of the
National Social Investment Office. The Program was
created to ensure a more equitable distribution
of resources to vulnerable populations including children,
youth, and women. Under President Muhammadu Buhari,
the office has created four programs to address poverty
and help increase economic development




Our Programmes
Our Programmes are specifically designed to empower,train and uplift millions of
abandoned Nigerians in every part of the country



Conditional Cash Transfer(CCT)

Nigeria's conditional cash transfer programs provide targeted cash transfers to the most vulnerable
household with the long term goal of lifting millions out of poverty
